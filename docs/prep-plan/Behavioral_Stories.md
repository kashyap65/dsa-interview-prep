## 📌 Story Preparation Tips

### The STAR-I Checklist (before any behavioral interview)
- [ ] Every story has a **specific metric** in the Result (%, time saved, volume, $)
- [ ] Every story is under **2.5 minutes** when spoken aloud
- [ ] Every story ends with **your personal contribution** — not "we did X"
- [ ] You have **2 versions** of each story: 90-second (phone screen) and 3-minute (panel)
- [ ] You know which **2 LPs** each story maps to (for Amazon rounds)
